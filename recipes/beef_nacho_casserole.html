Beef Nacho Casserole

Layers of seasoned ground beef, corn, and tortilla chips baked under a layer of Colby cheese!

This nacho casserole is a simple meal to make in less than an hour that even the kids are sure to love.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 1/2 cups chunky salsa
  • 1 (10 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 3/4 cup creamy salad dressing
    (such as Miracle Whip)
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 2 cups crushed tortilla chips
  • 2 cups Colby cheese

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C).
  2. Cook ground beef in a large beef skillet over medium-high heat until browned and
    crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Remove from the heat and drain.
  3. Stir salsa, corn, salad dressing, and chili powder into the skillet until well combined.
  4. Layer 1/2 of the ground beef mixture in an ungreased 2-quart casserole dish; top
    with 1/2 of the tortilla chips and 1/2 of the cheese. Repeat layers once more.
  5. Bake uncovered, in the preheated oven, until heted through and cheese is melted, about 20 minutes.
